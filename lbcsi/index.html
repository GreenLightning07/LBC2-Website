Legacy

This year LBCSI celebrates its 11th year. The Loyola Blakefield Cyber initiative has given many students everlasting opportunities and memories, as well as growing into a community of growth and community. This year also marks the 6th LBC2 student run cyber competition.

LBCSI is a student run initiative and directed by Steve Morrill, Head of Technology & Cyber Science at Loyola Blakefield. This past year, One of our teams made National Finals of CyberPatriot, and Steve Morrill recived the Coach of the Year award.
